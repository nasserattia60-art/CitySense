##🌍 CitySense

CitySense is a web platform that helps people understand the real pros, cons, and daily challenges of cities before visiting or relocating, using practical insights, trusted data, and community-reported real-time issues.

##💡 Inspiration

Many people choose cities based on attractive images or incomplete information.
CitySense was created to reveal the real everyday conditions of cities—helping people avoid unexpected problems and make confident, informed decisions.

##⚙️ What It Does

CitySense allows users to:
Explore cities realistically before moving or visiting
Understand real advantages, disadvantages, and recurring problems
Access practical insights related to cost of living, transportation, safety, and services
Avoid risks caused by outdated or misleading information

##🛠️ How We Built It

Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
Backend (planned): Django
UX Design: Card-based, interactive Hackathon-style interface
Architecture: Clear separation between UI, logic, and future data layer

##🚧 Challenges We Faced

Presenting city problems objectively without bias
Structuring large amounts of information clearly
Designing a simple yet scalable UX
Making critical information easy to access

##🏆 Accomplishments

Built a functional and interactive prototype
Designed a clean, expandable card-based UI
Created a foundation for real-time city intelligence
Focused on real-world impact, not just visuals

##📚 What We Learned

Transparency is critical for decision-based platforms
UX directly affects real-life choices
Simplicity improves trust and usability
Planning architecture early saves time later

##🚀 What’s Next

Integrating verified real-time data from trusted governmental and private-sector sources
Creating city-based communities where users can report live issues (closed bridges, snow, floods, etc.)
Improving data validation and accuracy mechanisms
Expanding decision-support features

##🌱 Social Impact

CitySense empowers people to make safer, smarter decisions about where they live or travel—reducing risks, saving time, and improving quality of life through transparent, real-world information.
