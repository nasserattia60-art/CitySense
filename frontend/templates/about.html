Inspiration

CitySense was inspired by a common real-life problem: many people move to new cities for study, work, or travel… only to discover unexpected issues like noise, insects, unsafe neighborhoods, or poor-quality water. There is no single trusted platform that gives a clear picture of the real living conditions in any area. I wanted to build a tool that empowers people to make smarter decisions before they move.

What it does

CitySense analyzes any city or neighborhood and generates a detailed AI-powered report that shows:

  • Noise levels
  • Air and water quality
  • Mosquito and insect density
  • Traffic and roadwork issues
  • Neighborhood safety
  • Average rent prices
  • Weather patterns
  • User reviews and local insights

Users can also enter a specific address, and the AI will generate a personalized risk and quality-of-life analysis within seconds.
